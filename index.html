Open the shop

Signature mechanic — try it

Payment has weight.

No coins. No floating numbers. The stones sit on one pan — you pour barley into the other until the beam believes you.

Hold the sack. Pour.

In game, your weight set grows with your standing — 1 · 2 · 5 · 10 · 25 · 50, and up.

The stones total 85. The beam does not lie.

Buying & inspection

Turn it over in your hands.

People bring you things — copper, seals, statues, a dead man’s tools. Weigh the piece. Read the surface. Name your price and defend it.

Early on, the truth is plain. Later, fakes learn to hide.

Pawn & collateral

Some customers do not come to buy.

They come to survive. Accept collateral, set terms, wait. If they return, it’s theirs. If they don’t, it’s yours.

A living economy

The city changes the price.

A drought changes barley. A festival changes demand. A caravan changes supply. A war changes everything.

  • Drought

    Grain thins. Tempers shorten.

  • Fertile Harvest

    Baskets overflow. Prices soften.

  • Festival

    Crowds spend. Shelves empty.

  • Disease

    Streets empty. Doors close.

  • Trade Caravan

    New goods. New rumors.

  • War

    Routes darken. Everything changes.
